Baked Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken: Most commonly, boneless, skinless chicken breasts are used for a lean option, but you can use thighs or whole chicken depending on the recipe.
  • Oil: Olive oil is the most common choice for its flavor and ability to help the chicken brown.
  • Seasoning: Salt, pepper, and garlic powder are essential seasonings, with additional herbs like rosemary, thyme, or oregano adding depth.
  • Marinades (optional): Some recipes might include a marinade with lemon juice, yogurt, or a combination of herbs and spices to enhance flavor and moisture.

  5. Place the chicken in a baking dish or on a sheet pan lined with parchment paper or foil for easy cleanup.
  6. Bake the chicken in the preheated oven for 20-30 minutes for boneless breasts or 35-45 minutes for bone-in pieces, depending on thickness.

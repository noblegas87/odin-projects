Lasanga

Ingredients:

  • Pasta: Lasagna noodles
  • Meat sauce: Ground beef (can be substituted with other meats) mixed with tomato sauce
  • Cheese: Ricotta cheese (often mixed with egg and Parmesan), shredded mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan cheese
  • Binders: Eggs (used in the ricotta mixture)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce by browning ground beef and mixing it with tomato sauce.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese with eggs, Parmesan, and seasonings for the cheese layer.
  5. In a baking dish, layer the ingredients: start with meat sauce, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella.
  6. Repeat the layering process until all ingredients are used, finishing with a top layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  7. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before serving.

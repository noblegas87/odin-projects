Creamy Cajun Chicken Pasta

Ingredients:

  • Pasta: Linguini or penne pasta works well
  • Chicken: Boneless, skinless chicken breast strips
  • Cajun seasoning: A blend that usually contains paprika, cayenne, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, thyme, and black pepper
  • Vegetables: Bell peppers, mushrooms, and green onions
  • Cream: Heavy cream
  • Cheese: Parmesan cheese
  • Other seasonings: Basil, lemon pepper, garlic powder, and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta until al dente
  2. Toss chicken with Cajun seasoning
  3. Saut√© chicken in butter until cooked through
  4. Add vegetables and cook for a few minutes
  5. Reduce heat and stir in heavy cream
  6. Season sauce and heat through
